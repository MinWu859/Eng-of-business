About Me

Hi my name is Min Wu and I am currently a senior at Penn State majoring in Digital Media. I have learned a lot of specialized knowledge in this major, such as text-Analysis, statistics, animation, and website creation. I think that learning these professional skills can potentially help me in my future career.

I have previously interned at two professional media companies. During my internship, I helped the companies plan the content of short videos and promos, created short video scripts, post-edited the promos, and researched how to increase the number of views and views of short videos. These internship projects gave me a deeper understanding of the media industry and helped me acquire many professional skills.

In my spare time, I like to play music and read books related to business studies. Piano and ukulele are my best instruments. I also enjoy reading the autobiographies of successful entrepreneurs and learning about their past events.

My goal is to create a big strategy game with my partners and start our own famous game company.
